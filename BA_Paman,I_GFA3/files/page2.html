Young people need all the information and knowledge to make healthy decisions. Join us and save many people against sexual violence.







Why Support Sex Education?




Children, having known nothing about sex and other stuff related to it are the most vulnerable to abuse. Research showed that children tend to believe and trust easily and adults tend to take advantage of that. Women aren't the only victims of sexual abuse, men have also experienced this.

According to Unicef, most often, abuse occurs at the hands of someone a child knows and trusts. At least 120 million girls under the age of 20 – about 1 in 10 – have been forced to engage in sex or perform other sexual acts, although the actual figure is likely much higher. Adults, with disgusting things going on inside their head make children think that the abuse they (children) are going through is just a game and it's fun, but the truth is it's not.

Children being taken advantage of by adults isn't the only reason why I support honest sex education. Sex education is also beneficial to teenagers. Teenagers curious about sex need to know the proper knowledge and skills in order to prevent unwanted pregnancies and getting STDs.
